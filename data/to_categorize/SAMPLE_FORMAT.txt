This is a sample file showing the expected CSV format for transaction files to be categorized.

Your CSV files in this directory should have these columns:

Date,Description,Amount,Appears On Your Statement As
2023-01-15,AMAZON.COM,25.99,AMZN Mktp US
2023-01-16,GROCERY STORE,52.47,SAFEWAY #1234
2023-01-17,NETFLIX.COM,-13.99,NETFLIX.COM

Notes:
- Date format should be YYYY-MM-DD
- Amount is a numeric value (negative for credits)
- "Appears On Your Statement As" column is optional but helpful for categorization
